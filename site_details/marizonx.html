SOLUTION

The solution focused on presenting the strongest work as
clearly and impactfully as possible.
High-quality video showcases were paired with an
interactive comparison slider, allowing viewers
to see side-by-side results of color-graded versus
ungraded footage, effectively highlighting the artist’s
expertise and attention to detail.

The design prioritizes immersion and clarity,
ensuring the work remains the focal point without unnecessary distractions.

INDUSTRY

Cinematography / Video Editing

OVERVIEW

The goal was to create a portfolio website
that showcases the cinematographer’s and
video editor’s visual storytelling skills,
with a strong emphasis on
color grading quality and creative precision.
